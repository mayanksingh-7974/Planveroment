# Planveroment - Smart Plant Care Assistant

Planveroment is a modern web application designed to help plant enthusiasts manage their indoor and outdoor greenery with ease. Built by the **Spam Bytes** team, it solves the problem of improper watering and sunlight management through data-driven insights and AI-powered plant identification.

- **Smart Plant Identification**: Uses advanced AI (GPT-5.2 Vision) to identify plants from text descriptions or image uploads.
- **Personalized Care Guides**: Automatically generates optimized watering schedules and sunlight requirements based on the specific plant species.
- **Watering Reminders**: Track the last time each plant was watered and see exactly when the next session is due.
- **Plant Library**: A curated list of common plants with detailed care instructions.
- **Health Tracking**: Monitor the health status of your balcony or indoor garden at a glance.
